About Me

A passion for web applications development, with an entrepreneur mindset fuelling my determination for analysing information and understand business processes, has enabled me to become a proficient and professional individual who can perform to the highest standards. Experience and skills attained over 6 years working in different industries ranging from Human Resources, Finance, Childcare and Hospitality and Tourism as a web developer/programmer and business analyst.

The autodidactic skills of a Developer and Analyst coupled with my experience in various industries have given me exposure, insight, and an acute ability to design and develop web applications that best support a given project using the best tools possible. Ability to define business processes and developing workflows by analysing the business needs and developing web applications to meet client requested features to support development, and planning and managing releases of the product and ensuring the quality of the product for customer acceptance, adoption, and success are parts of a growing skill-set.

The enthusiasm to gain knowledge and keep up-to-date with current technologies for development and maintaining quality products adds strength and competency to work in agile environments. As an enthusiast of the IT and Engineering sector am a graduate of Bachelor of Science with double majors in Computer Science and Electrical Electronics Engineering from the University of the South Pacific.

My mission in life is to make a difference where ever I am.
